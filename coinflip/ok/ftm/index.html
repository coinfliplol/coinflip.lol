How to Play Coinflip

Coinflip is a simple gambling game where a player bet on the outcome of a coin toss.

To play Coinflip, follow these steps:

  1. Choose your bet amount.
  2. Select your preferred side of the coin (bet heads or bet tails).
  3. A coin will be flipped, and the outcome (heads or tails) will determine if the player won or lost.
  4. The player wins 1.8x their bet size.

The winner of the Coinflip game is determined based on the outcome of the coin toss. If the side you chose matches the side of the coin that lands facing up, you win.
