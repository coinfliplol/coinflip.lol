How to Play Lottery

Lottery is a game of chance where players purchase tickets and participate in a random draw for the chance to win prizes.

To play Lottery, follow these steps:

  1. Purchase lottery tickets.
  2. Wait for the draw to take place. (Each started lottery has its own timer for when it ends)
  3. If you win your prize will be sent directly to your participant address by the smart contract.
  4. The one that ends the lottery (By clicking the end button after the lottery timer runs out); Earns 1% of the Prize Pool.

The winners of the Lottery game are determined based on a random process with different variables. There is 1 winner per Lottery.

Yes, Lottery is considered fair as the winning number is drawn randomly by the smart contract, and all tickets have an equal chance of winning.
